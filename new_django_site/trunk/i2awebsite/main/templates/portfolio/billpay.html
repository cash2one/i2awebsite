Challenge

Build a mobile and web-based tool to enable consumer the ability to pay their bills via a mobile app.

Technologies used

Solution

The Bill Pay app is a downloadable smart phone app and integrated with a hosted server platform. Together, this solution allows a consumer to pay a bill by photographing a bill, statement or invoice for scheduled payment. It allows consumers to leverage a variety of payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, bank accounts, online accounts, wallets and prepaid alternatives, giving consumers ease-of-use and freedom of choice.

It leverages smart-phone-enabled “snap and capture” capabilities, including identification using a Driver’s License, and linking with bank accounts by snapping a picture of a check.
