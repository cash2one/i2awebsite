We built the Swim Meet Mobile app to organize all the swim meet essentials, including:

  • meet program
  • event details
  • swimmer stats
  • heat sheets
  • timelines
  • lane assignments
  • up-to-the-minute results

The app transforms the swim meet experience for fans, swimmers, coaches and swim meet hosts alike.

Great for Fans

Fans can easily access swimmer, team and meet information using an advanced search function, save their favorite swimmers and teams to the app for quick access, and share meet results via email, messaging and social channels.

Useful for Swimmers and Coaches

Swimmers and coaches can also benefit by easily checking on event timelines, heats and lane assignments so there is no need to track down paper programs ever again.

Helpful for Meet Hosts

Meet hosts can keep everything organized from one central location by:

  • sharing estimated timelines
  • pushing out event schedules and psych sheet information
  • selling mobile heat sheets within the app for fans, swimmers and coaches prior to the meet

Swim fans, parents and participants can get all the information they need in real-time, eliminating any headache or confusion over the event details, so everyone can enjoy the excitement of the main event without hassle.
