Idea

Create a website to resell Tech Support for Dummies (TSD) services that integrates into the company’s web services.

Technologies used

Solution

In order for a company to effectively sell Tech Support for Dummies services (technical support contracts for all types of home computers and electronics) on the web, we developed a web-based application that could access TSD’s product offerings.

The site allows consumers to order either one-time support or signup for a subscription plan that includes 24x7 support. The solutions utilize by the web- based eCommerce website were also integrated into Saleforce.com and the company’s call center to offer consumers the option to also order the service via call center agents.
