Challenge

Create an ad platform that allows advertisers to send targeted ads to consumers who use travel-related web-based and mobile applications.

Technologies used

Solution

We built an easy to use self-service ad platform that allows businesses to target travel enthusiasts based on destinations and trip preferences. We created an easy-to-use system that developers of travel-based mobile and web-based app can use to register their apps for possible advertising opportunities. Advertisers utilize the system to send targeted ads to users of travel-related mobile apps or web-based apps. They can also set parameters for how much they want to spend on a campaign and use their own graphic or text-based banner ads.

In addition to specific travel parameters, advertisers can target ads to be delivered to users based on gender, age, and interest. Agencies will appreciate that the system works well with third party tools such as DART reporting.
