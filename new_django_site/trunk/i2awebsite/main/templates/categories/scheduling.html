Scheduling & Alerting Applications

Many companies, large and small, have unique needs for online scheduling applications or workforce management issues that a web or mobile applications will solve. i2a Solutions has worked diligently to provide custom development services where required to support these types of scenarios.

Many different types of applications

We have designed and developed many types of scheduling and alert applications.

  • Airline booking and scheduling applications
  • Scheduling medical appointments
  • Assuring patients take their medicine on time
  • Teaching children how to schedule their task and get awards
  • An enhanced scheduling system for home services
  • Time management system

Alerts & notifications

Our schedule solutions utilize many different types of way to alert and notify users of upcoming appointments or issues that have occurred. These range from:.

  • IVR systems to call the users with Text-to-Speech alerts
  • SMS text alerts with ability to send confirmations of receiving alerts
  • Emailing reminders
  • Desktop popup alerts

Integration to other online platforms

We have integrated our applications to many different types of 3rd party applications and devices including:

  • Many different types of calendaring applications (Google, Outlook, iCloud, etc.)
  • Social media sites (Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  • Devices (time clocks, medical devices, monitoring platforms)
