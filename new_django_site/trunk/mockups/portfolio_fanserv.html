Challenge

Create an ad platform that allows advertisers to send targeted ads to people who use sports related web-based and mobile applications.

Technologies used

Solution

We built an easy to use self-service ad platform that allows businesses to target sports enthusiasts based on team and league preferences. We created an easy- to-use system that mobile and web-based app developers can use to register their apps for possible advertising opportunities. Advertisers can use the system to send targeted ads to users of sports-related mobile apps or web-based apps. They can also set parameters for how much they want to spend on a campaign and use their own graphic or text-based banner ads..

In addition to league and team parameters, advertisers can target those with specific interests, age, gender and other options. Agencies will appreciate that the system works well with third party tools such as DART reporting.
