Who is i2aSolutions.com

At i2a Solutions we are experts at transforming client ideas into robust consumer and enterprise applications. Our proven team of experts has designed and developed over 250 software products ranging from mobile and web applications to large-scale enterprise class solutions. Our unique breadth of knowledge and experience means we understand system design and develop products that integrate seamlessly with your company's existing infrastructure. We know every product is unique and demands a customized design and development approach.

Our team of usability specialists and software engineers, working in collaboration with you and your company, will recommend a strategic and innovative design plan to meet the specific needs of your product as well as provide options that will support its long-term success. To ensure cost-effective and efficient solutions for our clients we use a blended team approach. Our U.S.-based design and management team is headquartered in San Diego, California and have additional engineering offices in Central Europe.
